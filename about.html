We are PeaksoundVA

PeaksoundVA was started in 2013 by Colin Mackey and David Barth. Inspired by their love for sound and a lucrative Craigslist ad, they have crawled on their hands and knees from lowly beginnings at the University of Mary Washington, to slightly less lowly status as a sound company. Colin and David, together with their friend Greg Chandler started working at various times on UMW's student sound crew. There their love for all aspects of the sound industry blossomed and evolved. It was David who inspired the group to create this company late in 2013.

We are Peak Sound VA

Peak Sound was started in 2013 by Colin Mackey and David Barth. Inspired by our love for sound and a lucrative Craigslist ad, we have crawled on our hands and knees from lowly beginnings at the University of Mary Washington, to slightly less lowly status as a sound company. Colin and David, together with our friend Greg Chandler started working at various times on UMW's student sound crew. There our love for all aspects of the sound industry blossomed and evolved. It was David who inspired us group to create this company late in 2013.

Pardon our dust! We're under construction!
